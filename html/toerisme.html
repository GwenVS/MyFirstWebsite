Holidays in Rhodes

Lindos Princess Beach Hotel is located in an ideal area for beach holidays in Rhodes near the famous and historic village of Lindos which is just 7 km away from this all inclusive resort and offers a fantastic opportunity for shopping, swimming and sightseeing and 1 km away from the village of Lardos. This family beach hotel is built in front of its beautiful sand and pebble private beach which is nominated with the ‘Blue Flag’; a demanding international program for environmental education, information and awareness. Furthermore, our beach hotel is 50 km away from the airport which can be reached either by bus, car or taxi, 55 km from the port of Rhodes and 55 km from Rhodes town which features an old town built in medieval times; a monument of global cultural heritage proclaimed by UNESCO and plenty other sightseeing’s. If that is not enough during your Rhodes holidays, the destination within a driving range of 50 minutes offers charming villages such as Asklipio, Lachania and Profilia, the famous Butterfly Valley, plenty of famous beaches and ‘Prassonisi’, a paradise for wind surfers and kite surfers.
Feel free to contact our Front Office in order to arrange a car, motorbike or bicycle rental and a taxi, while on holidays in Rhodes. In addition, conveniently just outside our all inclusive resort there is a bus stop. Rhodes Island is the capital of Dodecanese islands; due to its geographical location and the over-2400 year’s history this island has plenty of interesting stories, sights and secrets to discover during your holidays. Our local staff will be happy to assist you and organize for you any kind of information, excursions planning and activities you may like to experience.

Nightlife at Lindos

Lindos is an upcoming and world famous resort in southern Rhodes. The perfection of the resort is uniquely conveyed by the combination of magnificent architecture, spectacular clear beaches and extraordinary nightlife.
Nightlife in Lindos is vibrant and can be favorably compared to other destinations of the island famous for the nightlife options they offer. The settlement stays "alive" until the wee hours and promises beautiful nights below the shadow of the imposing and stunning castle of Lindos.
Lindos is brimming with bars, restaurants and nightclubs. Enjoy your evening, strolling around the picturesque streets of the village and taste the gastronomy of Rhodes by enjoying the breathtaking view in one of the many restaurants that have roof top gardens. Next, you can continue your evening with a refreshing cocktail at a chic nightclub.
Whatever your mood is, there are a lot of available venues for entertainment, whether you prefer intense and extreme nights or more relaxed. Do not miss the opportunity to experience the nightlife of Lindos during your summer vacation in Rhodes.
