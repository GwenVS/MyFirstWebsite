Swimming pool

At our family beach hotel here are three spacious outdoor swimming pools with separate children’s pool and a pool with water slides. Umbrellas, sun beds and beach towels are available free of charge. In addition, there is an indoor pool in the hotel’s wellness center. There is normally a Life Guard around the pool areas, however, please note that the hotel gives a sign with a flag when the Life Guard is not on duty. Parents are kindly requested to take care of their children when using the swimming pools.

The Beach

The hotel’s beach is awarded with a the Blue Flag which means that it has fulfilled the high criteria for water quality, safety, environmental education and information, the provision of services and general environmental management criteria. We take pride in keeping the beach clean so please do not throw any garbage directly on the beach. Sundecks and umbrellas are available, free of charge.

Wellness Center

Holidays are synonymous with rest and relaxation and the ideal place to experience these qualities is a spa resort. To really wash away the stress and strains of your daily life, why not explore Lindos Princess Wellness Center? In our spa resort you can choose from a number of spa treatments; skilled therapists will refresh your mind, body and spirit for a perfect pampering. Spoil yourself with free use of sauna, Jacuzzi and Hamam or with a dive into our heated indoor swimming pool with fresh water leaving you glowing inside out Lindos Princess as a genuine spa all inclusive resort wouldn't dissatisfy the exercise fans. If you are one of those daily holiday work-out enthusiasts or just want some exercise to add up for all those servings in the Snack Bar, you don’t have to leave our grounds for a gentle, or not so gentle, exercise. Leander Fitness Center is located at the Ground Floor. The gym is fully equipped and is a part of the all- inclusive system. For safety reasons, children under 15 years old are not allowed entrance.
