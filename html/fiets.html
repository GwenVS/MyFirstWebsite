Our Animation Team is responsible for the entertainment program during your stay. There are plenty of different activities all day long for people in all ages. The hotel also offers Greek Evening entertainment with live orchestra and a number of shows for our guests.

Lindo & Linda’s Mini Club

With supervision of trained staff, children from 4 – 12 years old can take part in leisure activities from Mon – Sun. 10:00 -12:00 hrs and 14:30 to 17:30. The popular Mini Club provides separate playgrounds in the hotel area. Lindo & Linda are two very special donkeys coming to the hotel every day to play with all the children in the Mini Club. Lindo & Linda loves to tell the children exiting stories and teach them funny dances!

All Inclusive Sports

Here at Lindos Princess Beach Hotel you can enjoy a variety of sports activities; Tennis, basket, football, water polo, beach volley, aerobics, water gymnastic, archery, mini-golf, boccia and table tennis.

Water Sport Center

The Water Sports Center offers all kinds of water fun such as Windsurfing, Cat Sailing, Water-Ski, Wakeboard, Jet Ski, Banana Boat, Pedalos, Ringos, Crazy Sofa and Canoe. The centre is located at the Beach and is open Monday – Sunday.Partner on site
www.watersports-lindos.com

Video Game Room

The Video Game Room is located next to the Wellness Center and it is open 24 hrs. Here you can play Pool, Junior Basket Dream Shooters, Video Games, Photo Play, football table, air hockey and so on.

Hairdresser

Take advantage of our Hair Salon where you can cut, blow-dry, tint or completely re-style your hair. A number of different manicure and pedicure are also available.
