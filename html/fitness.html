Conferences & Meetings

Our beachfront location and our excellent conference facilities make Lindos Princess Beach Hotel in Rhodes an outstanding location for your meeting, event or conference. The beach hotel’s multi-purpose conference facilities offer great opportunities for meetings and events under the Mediterranean Sun.

Designed with your comfort and requests in mind, the high ceiled conference room “ Clio” (980 m² ) has a capacity to host 500 people. Conveniently located outside “Clio”, the hotel’s sea view roof veranda invites for a perfect canapé break or a cocktail party – you decide! The conference rooms “Eyridiki” (110 m²) and “Electra” (115 m²) open their doors for more intimate meetings or events with a capacity for 80 persons each.

The conference rooms of this all inclusive resort are fully equipped with advanced communications systems and audio-visual equipment. All our meeting rooms have WiFi access so that our guests can be always connected. Apart from our facilities our management team will always be by your side to make sure that all needs and expectations are met. In order to arrange the perfect meeting or event with us feel free to contact us in events@hhotels.gr.
